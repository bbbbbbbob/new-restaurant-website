Nestled in the heart of Duluth, Georgia, Saigon District Kitchen and Bar brings the vibrant flavors of Vietnam to the Atlanta metro area. Our kitchen celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Saigon — from slow-simmered pho and crisp banh mi to sizzling plates of lemongrass chicken and fresh spring rolls. Whether you are joining us for a casual lunch, a lively dinner with friends, or a weekend brunch, every dish is prepared with authentic recipes and the finest ingredients. We take pride in being a gathering place for the Duluth community, where warm hospitality meets bold, unforgettable Vietnamese cuisine. Come experience the taste of Saigon right here in Gwinnett County.
